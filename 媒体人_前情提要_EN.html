Language Change
Abstract
You are the owner of a Media Center.
Early in the morning, you learned a piece of heavy news: British princess Susannah was kidnapped. The kidnapper’s request was that the prime minister had to have sex with a pig at 4 o’clock that afternoon and broadcast it to the world live, otherwise the princess would be killed.
The government’s public relations department quickly ordered a ban on media coverage. When you were wondering whether to publish this news to get huge traffic, you received a videotape and a severed finger provided by the kidnapper.
The contents of the video tape show that the princess's finger was cut off by the kidnappers.

Please make your choice:

Web traffic is here, I want to send the video
Follow the rules and don’t send the video
